Associate Editors Recruitment

If you've ever mused about how a centralized international tax system would work, or even casually wondered how international economic institutions affect global food security, we're the Journal for you.

Associate Editors act as the gatekeepers of the Journal. They meet in small groups and discuss and dissect the submissions in order to determine which articles are worthy of possible publication. They pass papers on to the Senior Board that they deem worthy and write constructive rejection letters to the authors that are not accepted. Those who are enthusiastic can also offer to assist Staff Editors to conduct footnote checks for the few papers that are ultimately approved.

To apply to be an associate editor, please send an email to editor@jilir.org indicating your interest by September 18, 2014. Cover letter and résumé are not necessary.

Baker Lecture on “Food Security, the World Trade Organisation, and the Social State”

Date: Tuesday, September 16, 2014
Time: 4:00pm to 6:00pm
Location: Solarium Falconer Hall 84 Queen's Park

The Baker Lecture on “Food Security, the World Trade Organisation, and the Social State” will be delivered by Professor Anne Orford. The lecture will sketch the re-emergence of food security as a major global problem and project over the past decade, one that has significant legal dimensions.

As part of a symposium, the JILIR will be publishing Professor Orford’s distinguished lecture. We will also be publishing short commentaries that will respond to the lecture and provide multiple perspectives in which to analyse the issue of global food security.
